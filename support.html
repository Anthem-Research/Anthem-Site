Support

Support Anthem’s independent research, projects, fieldwork, writing, and software experiments.

Anthem is supported through patronage, research access, organisational sponsorship, research partnerships, and defined consulting engagements.

I

Supporter

$9/month

Individual patronage for Anthem’s independent research, public development, fieldwork, writing, software experiments, and long-form project formation.

This is not a fixed content subscription. Notes, updates, fragments, previews, invitations, or field material may be shared when they naturally exist.

II

Research

$440/month

Access for individuals, researchers, and small builders who want proximity to selected live projects at the build layer.

Includes selected repositories, specifications, protocols, build logs, working documents, early frameworks, and research-use fork rights under defined terms. This is not a content tier.

III

Sponsor

$500–$1,500/month

Organisation-level patronage for aligned companies, studios, foundations, land projects, DAOs, agencies, and institutions.

Includes public acknowledgement, optional sponsor listing, occasional sponsor updates, and selected invitations. Sponsorship does not include full research access, ownership, editorial control, or commercial rights.

IV

Research Partner

$2,000–$5,000/month

Organisation-level research access for companies and institutions that want structured proximity to the work itself.

Includes private briefings, selected repository or framework access, early research visibility, and limited internal-use rights where agreed in writing.

V

Consult

$6,000+

A defined applied engagement around a specific strategic or structural problem.

Relevant areas include land project structure, governance design, AI workflow design, blockchain coordination, brand or lab positioning, research direction, and protocol architecture.
