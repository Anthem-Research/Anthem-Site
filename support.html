Support

Support Anthem.

Anthem is an independent research lab and studio. Support is structured through patronage, research access, organisational sponsorship, research partnership, and defined consulting engagements.

I

Supporter

$9/month

Individual patronage for Anthem’s independent research, fieldwork, writing, software experiments, and public project development.

No fixed content schedule. Notes, updates, fragments, previews, invitations, or field material may be shared when they naturally exist.

II

Research

$440/month

Access for individuals, researchers, and small builders who want proximity to selected live projects at the build layer.

Includes selected repositories, specifications, protocols, build logs, working documents, early frameworks, and research-use fork rights under defined terms. Not a content tier.

III

Sponsor

$500–$1,500/month

Organisation-level patronage for aligned companies, studios, foundations, land projects, DAOs, agencies, and institutions.

Includes public acknowledgement, optional sponsor listing, occasional sponsor updates, and selected invitations. No full research access, ownership, editorial control, or commercial rights.

IV

Research Partner

$2,000–$5,000/month

Organisation-level research access for companies and institutions that want structured proximity to the work itself.

Includes private briefings, selected repository or framework access, early research visibility, and limited internal-use rights where agreed in writing.

V

Consult

$6,000+

A defined applied engagement around a specific strategic or structural problem.

Relevant areas include land project structure, governance design, AI workflow design, blockchain coordination, brand or lab positioning, research direction, and protocol architecture.
