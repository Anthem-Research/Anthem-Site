Commission

Fixed-scope pilot studies.

Anthem works through bounded studies rather than open-ended consultancy. Each pilot produces a defined artefact: dossier, protocol, charter, map, schema, or implementation memo.

I

ARCA Provenance Sprint

From $3,500
15 working days

For premium product brands, textile mills, object studios, exporters, and makers that need clearer material evidence and product-level records.

Deliverables include an evidence checklist, material or supply-chain map, 10–15 page provenance dossier, DPP-ready field schema, prototype passport page, and claims gap memo.

II

Bloom Site Intelligence Baseline

From $3,000
15–20 working days

For landowners, regenerative farms, eco-lodges, conservation sites, estates, and site-based projects that need a structured operating picture of place.

Deliverables include a site register, zone map, stewardship calendar, access and restoration matrix, risk memo, field observation template, and optional operating dashboard.

III

Arena Governance Charter Sprint

From $4,000
20 working days

For site owners, residency directors, boards, collectives, field stations, DAOs, and physical projects with a real multi-party governance problem.

Deliverables include stakeholder map, membership and authority matrix, contribution and treasury flows, dispute and exit logic, draft charter, records architecture, and implementation memo.
