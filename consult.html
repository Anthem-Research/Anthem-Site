Consult

Anthem accepts selected commissions for research, writing, protocol design, and systems architecture.

Work may take the form of a research brief, whitepaper, project thesis, provenance framework, field-record system, operating protocol, or advisory retainer.

Consulting is suited to artists, founders, studios, small brands, land projects, cultural institutions, and technical teams developing work at the boundary of material systems, ecology, computation, and coordination.

Typical engagements include whitepaper creation, project framing, public research briefs, product and material provenance systems, field or site registers, coordination protocols, governance diagrams, repository structuring, and ongoing research advisory.

Anthem does not take on general branding, marketing, social media, or execution-only production work. The emphasis is on conceptual structure, evidence systems, design-research logic, and the operating grammar of a project.
