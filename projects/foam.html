Project

FOAM

A field observation frame for terrain, species, routes, water, access, and local environmental change.

Question

How can field records become operational intelligence for design, conservation, land use, and ecological work?

Position

FOAM treats observation as infrastructure. Notes, tracks, routes, water points, and local change become a structured record of a living site.

Surface

The system can produce maps, logs, site records, field protocols, route notes, and evidence surfaces for projects that operate within real terrain.

Status

FOAM currently exists as a working repository and field-system concept for ecological and place-based records.
