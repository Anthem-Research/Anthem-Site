Project

Proof of Labour

A material record system for objects, processes, makers, repairs, durability, and production evidence.

Question

How can an object carry a clear record of its material origin, production process, and continuing use?

Position

The project treats provenance as part of design. Evidence, process, and care become part of the identity of the object.

Surface

The system can hold material notes, process logs, maker records, repair histories, product pages, and public proof pages.

Status

This work develops from the earlier ARCA provenance frame and is moving toward a small product-led implementation.
