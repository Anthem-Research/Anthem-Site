About

A private studio-lab.

Anthem is an independent research and design studio based in Plettenberg Bay, South Africa. It works across systems, protocols, site intelligence, material provenance, ecological records, software experiments, and coordinated physical environments.

I

Method

Research into structure

Anthem develops work through field notes, diagrams, protocols, documents, repositories, charters, dossiers, and small interfaces. The aim is to turn complex concepts into operational forms without flattening their intellectual or aesthetic character.

II

Practice

Independent

The studio is intentionally small. Projects are developed directly, with specialist collaborators added only where a sold engagement or research object requires deeper technical, ecological, legal, or design capacity.
